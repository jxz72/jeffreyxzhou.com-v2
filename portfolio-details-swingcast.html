SwingCast

iOS app that uses AI to automatically track the swing of a golfer to record and display a video of their swing on their device and/or TV through Google Chromecast. Allows users to synchronize multiple devices to simultaneously record from different angles.

SwingCast helps over 400 golfers streamline their practice routines and add analytics to their game.

Currently working on porting SwingCast from Swift to Flutter/Dart/Kotlin to expand to Android devices.
